# Retail-Sales-analytics-SQL-
## Objective
Analyse sales data from a small trade platform to understand monthly revenue trends, product performance, and customer contribution.
## Tools used
- SQL
- Tableau
## Dataset
- Contains three tables:
- Customers table –Contains information about buyers such as customer ID, Name , etc.
- Products table –Details about different products and their category.
- Orders table –Records Order date, customer ID, quantity sold, sales done, etc.
## Key Insights
- Monthly revenue is inconsistent, peaking in Oct 2024, recovering in Jan 2025 and dropping sharply in Feb 2025.
- Customer spending is fairly distributed. Amit Sharma is top revenue-generating customer, while Pooja Nair contributes the least.
- Product revenue is balanced across items. Office chairs, wireless mouse, monitor stand generate highest revenue while table organizer and pen set generate the lowest.
- The Southern region shows relatively more consistent revenue compared to other regions.
